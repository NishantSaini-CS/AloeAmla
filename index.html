Our Story

About AloeAmla

A simple, transparent approach to holistic wellness and organic agriculture.

Our Farming Philosophy

At AloeAmla, we believe in working with nature, not against it. We use a method called Integrated Farming. Instead of planting only one crop on a piece of land, we grow Aloe Vera and Amla together on the same farm.

Amla trees grow tall and provide a gentle shade, which keeps the soil cool and preserves water. Aloe Vera plants grow happily underneath them, needing very little water and helping to prevent weeds from spreading. This natural partnership creates a rich, chemical-free ecosystem that helps both crops flourish without synthetic fertilizers or pesticides.

Connecting Directly with You

Traditional farming usually involves many middle agents who take a share of the profit, leaving the farmers with very little and raising prices for the customers. AloeAmla changes this.

By using our website, you connect directly with us. Whether you are a retail customer buying fresh gel, a farmer looking to grow under our guidance, or a retailer sourcing raw materials in bulk, we manage everything from our fields straight to your door step. This ensures fresh quality, reasonable prices, and fair pay for our growers.

How Our Integrated Farming Works

01

Tall Amla Trees

Amla trees are planted in wide rows. They grow tall, catching sunlight and acting as natural windbreaks while providing soft shade.

02

Understory Aloe Vera

Aloe Vera is planted in the spaces between rows. Because of the shade, the soil stays moist longer, saving water by up to 40%.

03

Organic Synergy

We use cow manure, bio-compost, and natural neem sprays. Both crops feed each other, making the soil healthy and products highly potent.
